# California Unclaimed Property – Heir Document Checklist

**Property:** ROGERS MACK R – $4,003.83 (Bank of America Checking Account)  
**Property ID:** 1034134399  
**State:** California

Use this checklist when filing as an heir. The California State Controller’s Office requires proof of death and your legal right to claim.

### Required Documents (Minimum)

| # | Document | Required For | Notes |
|---|----------|--------------|-------|
| 1 | Certified Death Certificate | All heir claims | Must be original or certified copy (issued within last 6 months preferred) |
| 2 | Government-issued Photo ID | All claimants | Driver’s license, passport, or state ID of the person filing |
| 3 | Proof of Heirship / Relationship | All heir claims | Examples: birth certificate, marriage certificate, or court documents showing you are a child, spouse, sibling, or other heir |
| 4 | Completed Claim Form | All claims | Download from sco.ca.gov or use the online portal |
| 5 | Affidavit of Heirship (if no probate) | HEIR - INTESTATE | Notarized statement listing all heirs and their relationship to the deceased |
| 6 | Letters Testamentary or Letters of Administration | HEIR - WILL or Court Appointed | If probate was opened |
| 7 | Copy of Will (if applicable) | HEIR - WILL | Certified copy if available |
| 8 | Trust Documents | HEIR - TRUST | Certificate of Trust or relevant pages showing you are Trustee |

### Additional Documents That May Be Requested

- Court order or Final Decree (for HEIR - FINAL DECREE)
- Proof of name change (marriage certificate, court order)
- Social Security card or proof of SSN of the deceased (sometimes requested)
- Bank statements or other supporting documents showing the account belonged to the deceased

### Recommended Order of Preparation

1. Obtain certified death certificate
2. Gather proof of your relationship to the deceased
3. Complete the Claim Form (select correct Claimant Relationship)
4. Have Affidavit of Heirship notarized (if intestate)
5. Submit everything together through the California Unclaimed Property portal or by mail

### Tips for Success

- Submit **original or certified copies** when possible.
- Keep copies of everything you send.
- If filing online, scan documents clearly.
- Response time from California is typically 60–90 days.
